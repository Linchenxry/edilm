About Edilm

Edilm is an innovative packaging material made from natural biopolymers like sodium alginate and gelatin. It is designed to dissolve completely in hot water, making it a sustainable and waste-free solution for seasoning packaging.

Our Product: BioSachet

BioSachet is our flagship edible film product. It is safe, food-grade, and can be consumed along with your meal. No need to open the packet — just drop it in boiling water and enjoy!
