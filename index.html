About Edilm

Edilm is an innovative packaging material made from seaweed and gelatin. It is designed to dissolve completely in hot water, making it a convenient, sustainable and waste-free solution for seasoning packaging.

Our Product

It is safe, food-grade, and can be consumed along with your meal. No need to open the packet — just drop it in boiling water and enjoy!
